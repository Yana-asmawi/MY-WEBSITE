Joomla! is one of world's most popular software packages.

It is used to build, organise, manage and publish content for

mall businesses, governments, non-profits and large organisations worldwide.

Features of Joomla!

1.Management of content:it help user to create and publish content;it supports WYSIWYG type of content creation, where you can see how it will look before it goes online.

2.User Management:it can supports the creation of multiple user accounts

3.Media Manager:it allows user to skip using FTP to upload media to the site

4.Supports for Templates:You can get templates from third parties, too, and once added to the site; you can apply a template sitewide or just for one section of the site.

Advantages

Its free:Joomla is an open source platform that is free for anyone to use

Easy to Host:It needs PHP and will work with MySQL and PostgreSQL databases. Joomla will happily run in most hosting environments, and you should have no problems finding a provider for your site

Easy to use:Joomla is often accused of being too complex for complete beginners. Indeed, some of the newer site builders, especially those offered under the Software-as-a-Service model, are more novice-proof.
